Site Plan

Talamban Chamber of Commerce

Site Overview

The Talamban Chamber of Commerce website will serve as a central hub for local businesses, fostering economic growth and community engagement in Barangay Talamban, Cebu City.

Proposed domain: talambanchamber.com

Purpose

The website aims to:

  • Provide resources and support for local businesses.
  • Host an events calendar for networking and workshops.
  • Maintain a business directory to encourage local shopping.
  • Promote economic growth and community collaboration.

Target Scenarios

  • Business Owners: Access membership benefits, fees, and application details.
  • Community Members: Browse and register for local events and workshops.
  • Potential Investors: Explore the local business environment and opportunities.

Design Elements

Color Scheme

Secondary Color: #34495e

Text Color: #ecf0f1

Typography
